The IBAT College Diploma in Web Design is a ten-week learning journey that leads you from the basics of HTML5 and CSS3 to building a personal portfolio website.

Classes are delivered live online one evening per week, from 6:30 to 9:00 pm. Students have access to our web-based learning resources, including notes, and exercise and project files. Individual student support, feedback and mentoring are provided by email.

Summary of course content:

  • Understand the basic structure of HTML5 web pages and CSS3 stylesheets.
  • Get to know with the software used every day by web designers: VS Code, GitHub, and DevTools.
  • Design visually attractive web layouts by adding images, colours, fonts, icons, and multi-column grids.
  • Create web pages that display responsively on user devices of every type - mobiles, tablets, desktops, and laptops.
  • Deploy websites from your computer to online web hosting services, and track user visits and interaction.
  • Gain the confidence to explore related career areas - such as e-commerce, UI/UX interface design, and front-end development.
  • Build a high-quality portfolio website that showcases your skills to future employers and clients.
  • Validate your web design experience and expertise with a recognised professional qualification.
