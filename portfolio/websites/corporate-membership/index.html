Join us.
Bring everyone with you.

When you join us, every employee in your organisation is enrolled as a member of our network – with full access to all the benefits of individual membership.

Up to 20% training discount: Spreadsheets for your accounts department, social media advertising for marketing, PRINCE® for project managers, data protection certification for your compliance team.

Our broad range of training programmes offers something for everyone at every level in your organisation.

Event Discounts: Members can avail of free or discounted access to more than 60 non-training events annually.

These include breakfast and lunchtime briefings, half-day workshops, evening seminars and social gatherings.

Online resources: Every month we add about ten new videos, presentations and documents to our members-only online library.

Staff development: Corporate membership offers a cost-effective yet high-impact method of delivering upskilling to your employees.
