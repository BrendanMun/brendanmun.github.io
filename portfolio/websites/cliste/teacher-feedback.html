What Teachers Are Saying About CLISTE

The children really enjoyed taking part in the CLISTE. It was active, fun and hands-on! The lesson content was age appropriate and targeted the interests of the modern day child.

CLISTE takes an imaginative approach to teaching children about internet safety and ICT skills while at the same time incorporating a huge range of integration among other curriculum areas. The children definitely enjoyed it!

Mary Devine
6th Class Teacher,
St. John's National School, Ballybrack,
Co. Dublin

I found the CLISTE programme very enjoyable to use.

The class found the information very relevant.

We focused on the staying safe lesson, and the slides and worksheets were very accessible.

The children particularly enjoyed the role play.

It was lots of fun.

Leah Duffy
Resource Teacher,
St. John's National School, Ballybrack,
Co. Dublin
