Who should attend?

itSMF Ireland is a not-for-profit organisation affiliated to itSMF International, a global membership organisation, established to promote and develop best practice in IT Service Management.

Since 1991, itSMF Ireland has been a prominent player in the on-going development and promotion of IT Service Management best practice, standards and qualifications.

Previous roles representing their organisations included:

  • IT Service Managers
  • Heads of IT
  • Help Desk Managers
  • Customer Service Managers
  • Project Managers
  • Members of itSMF Ireland and many more
