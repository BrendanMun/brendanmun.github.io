 Panel Discussion

Mary Cleary of ICS Skills will lead a 40-minute discussion among six key thought-leaders on the broad topic of Embracing Digital Age Learning – what does it mean for education, the economy and Irish society generally?
     Some conversation-starter topics are listed below.

Hear the Panel Discussion

Digital Literacy in 2015 Are we all digitally literate now?
Perhaps some more than others?
The ICT Skills Gap What does it look like in 2015? How can we tackle it?
ICT Skills #1 & #2 What's top of employers' list? And in second place?
ICT After 2nd-Level? Are school-leavers ICT-ready for further and higher levels? For employment?
Education, Meet ICT How best can we integrate ICT with learning - at every level?
Your Personal ICT Strategy What's most important for schools? What are the obstacles? How can they be overcome?
