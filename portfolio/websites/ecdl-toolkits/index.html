Why ECDL Certification?

Why certification?

Certification proves you have the skills to work to a recognised standard. Certification offers the following benefits:

  • ECDL equips you with practical computer and Internet skills
  • ECDL offers of a wide range of subject areas called Modules. So there is something for everybody – whatever your particular focus or career ambition
  • You can take individual Modules at your own pace, and earn an ECDL Certificate as you complete each one.

Why ECDL?

It’s quality, value and relevance have made ECDL the most popular IT certification in Ireland and worldwide.

  • Employers trust ECDL to provide the skills needed by the workforce, today and tomorrow
  • ECDL is Implemented according to strict quality assurance standards
  • ECDL is suitable for all skills levels
  • ECDL is up-to-date, relevant and in line with market needs and technological advancements

ECDL Profiles: for the evolving world of work

ECDL Profiles are personalised combinations of ECDL certifications which recognise that employees use technology for different reasons and at different levels of proficiency.

ECDL Profiles enable staff to achieve certification for those skills that most suit their needs or interests as they pursue their professional journey.

Individuals can grow their ECDL Profile over time as they update their skills, as technology evolves and as new certifications are developed.

Digital Marketer

I write copy daily and I regularly have to deliver perfect presentations. Web and image editing are vital in my job and we collaborate with clients in various time zones all over the world. This is my ECDL Profile.

Accounts Assistant

I use spreadsheets daily. I was surprised by how much I didn’t know about databases, or the advanced calculation in spreadsheets, and how they could help me in my day-to-day tasks. This is my ECDL Profile:

HR Manager

I’ve saved time writing reports, and researching strategies and best practices online. I wanted to be sure that I was protecting our employees’ data, so IT Security was also important to me. This is my ECDL Profile.

ECDL Modules and Toolkits

ECDL Training Toolkits are available for the following ECDL modules, each one of which provides a practical programme of up-to-date skills and knowledge areas.

ECDL Computer Essentials

Start here if you’re starting out. Learn the basics of computer usage, files and data security.

ECDL Online Essentials

Learn how to use the web and email, find the information you need, and stay secure online.

ECDL Word Processing

How to create, edit and format professional-standard documents with tables and images.

ECDL Spreadsheets

How to use a spreadsheet to record, update, lay out, analyse, graph and create reports on data.

ECDL Presentation

How to create audience-winning slideshows that include text, images, animation and videos.

ECDL Using Databases

How to use a database application to input, securely store and easily find and report on information.

ECDL IT Security

How to protect against hacking and theft, and evaluate security products and solutions.

ECDL Online Collaboration

Working smarter with collaborative tools such as productivity apps, social media and calendars.

ECDL Image Editing

How to use image editing apps to enhance images, apply effects, and prepare images for printing.

ECDL Web Editing

How to use HTML, CSS and images to build, update and publish websites on the Internet.

ECDL-endorsed Data Protection

How Ireland’s Data Protection Acts impact the way organisations must handle client information.

Now do I achieve ECDL Certification?

ECDL offers a range of learning pathways to suit every life situation. Your three main options to achieve ECDL certification are as follows:

Self-study online

Purchase an ECDL Training Toolkit. Each Toolkit contains everything you need to get certified in your chosen module(s).

With ECDL Toolkits you can learn new skills online at your own pace, wherever and whenever is convenient. Purchase an ECDL Toolkit

Attend a public course

Full and part-time classroom-based courses are available from our network of ECDL-accredited training providers across Ireland.

Your nearest ECDL Centre is probably a lot closer than you think. Find an ECDL- accredited training provider near you

Certification only

If you feel you already have the relevant skills and want to fast-track to certification, many of our ECDL-accredited test centres offer 45-minute ‘walk-in’ tests.

For details of testing schedules and prices, contact your nearest ECDL test centre
